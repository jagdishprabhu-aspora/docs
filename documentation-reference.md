1. KYC and Verification
United Kingdom

Documents Required:

Passport

Indian passport with at least 6 months validity

For other passports: Indian birthplace required

Live selfie

Status in AlphaDesk:

Approved leads to auto update

Pending or Not Approved leads to manual KYC review

Common Delay Reasons:

Mismatch between documents and selfie

Multiple accounts detected

Expired passport or invalid selfie

Region not acceptable

United Arab Emirates

Documents Required:

Emirates ID with at least 3 months validity

Live selfie

Status:

Verified via EFR

Common Delay Reasons:

Customer not accepted due to compliance

User located outside UAE

2. Referral Queries
United Kingdom

Rewards:

Referrer: £50

Referee: £20

Requirement:

Referee must complete £1,000 cumulative transfers

Key Notes:

No time limit

Referral link must be used during registration

Redemption begins after £100 transfers

United Arab Emirates

Rewards:

Referrer: 50 AED

Referee: 50 AED

Requirement:

5,000 AED cumulative transfers

Redemption:

Minimum 2,500 AED per transfer

Maximum 50 AED

Notes:

Referral link must be used during registration

Additional

Aspora Cash and Referral Lookup

User ID → Customer → Aspora Cash → Credit
