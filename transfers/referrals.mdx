## Overview

Help customers earn rewards by explaining how the Aspora referral program works. You'll learn the reward structures, requirements, and how to check referral status for UK, EUR, UAE, and USA customers.

## United Kingdom

### Reward Structure

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Referrer Reward" icon="gift">
    **£50**
    
    Paid to the person who shares the referral link
  </Card>
  <Card title="Referee Reward" icon="coins">
    **£20**
    
    Paid to the person who signs up using the link
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### How to Qualify for Rewards

Explain these requirements to customers:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Use referral link during registration">
    The referee must click the referral link and complete registration through it.
    
    <Warning>
    Referral links cannot be applied after registration. Ensure customers use the link when first signing up.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete cumulative transfers">
    The referee must complete **£1,000** in total transfers (can be spread across multiple transactions).
    
    <Check>
    Track progress in Alpha Desk under Customer → Aspora Cash → Credit
    </Check>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start earning rewards">
    Rewards begin accruing after the first **£100** in transfers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Key Notes

<Note>
  - **No time limit** - Referees can take as long as needed to complete the required transfers
  - Referral link **must be used during registration** - Cannot be applied retroactively
  - Invite via WhatsApp / text
  - Redemption begins at **≥ £100 transfers**
</Note>

### Redemption Details

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="money-bill" title="Minimum Transfer Amount">
    Each transfer must be at least **2,500 AED** to count toward redemption

  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>

</Warning>

### Adding Aspora Cash & Referral Lookup (UAE)

Use this path to check referral credits and Aspora Cash balance for UAE customers:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access User ID">
    Navigate to the customer profile using their User ID in Alpha Desk.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Go to Customer Section">
    Select the **Customer** tab in the profile navigation.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Aspora Cash">
    Click on **Aspora Cash** to view the current balance.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check Credit">
    Review the **Credit** section for referral rewards breakdown.
    
    <Check>
    The path is: User ID → Customer → Aspora Cash → Credit
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How to Check Referral Status (All Regions)

Use these steps to verify a customer's referral credits and Aspora Cash balance:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open customer profile">
    Navigate to the customer profile in Alpha Desk using their User ID.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access Customer section">
    Click the **Customer** tab in the profile navigation.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Aspora Cash balance">
    Select **Aspora Cash** to display the current balance.
    
    <Check>
    You'll see the total balance including referral rewards and other credits.
    </Check>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review credit details">
    Check the **Credit** section to see the breakdown of referral rewards earned.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Keep the Alpha Desk page open while on customer calls to quickly verify referral status and answer questions in real-time.
</Tip>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="question" title="Can a referral code be applied after registration?">
    No, the referral link must be used during the initial registration process. It cannot be applied retroactively.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="question" title="Is there a time limit to complete transfers?">
    For UK, UAE, and USA customers, there is no time limit. Referees can take as long as needed to complete the required transfers.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="question" title="What if the referee doesn't complete the required transfers?">
    Neither party will receive rewards until the requirement is met. For UK and UAE, this means cumulative transfers. For USA, it means a single $500 transfer.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="question" title="Can US customers use cumulative transfers like UK/UAE?">
    No, US referrals require a single transfer of $500, not cumulative transfers. This is different from UK and UAE requirements.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="question" title="How do I check if a customer used a referral link?">
    Check the customer's profile in Alpha Desk. If they used a referral link during registration, you'll see referral tracking information in their account details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
